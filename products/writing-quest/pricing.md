# Writing Quest Pricing

## Hypothesis

One-time paid license with early access discount.

- Early access paid beta: $19.
- Launch price: $29.
- No subscription.
- No account requirement.
- Early buyers get all 1.x updates.

This keeps the purchase impulse-friendly while still signaling that Writing Quest is a real paid writing tool, not a toy download.

## Questions

- Should there be a free trial?
- Should direct sales start through Gumroad, itch.io, or both?
- Should Steam wait until after direct beta signal?
- What refund/support promise is simple enough for a solo founder?

## Revisit Triggers

- Beta testers say the price feels unserious or too high for the current polish.
- The chosen sales channel changes fee or license-key expectations.
- A launch bundle or seasonal promo creates a better anchor.
