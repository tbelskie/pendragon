# Writing Quest

Retro writing desktop app for indie writers and hobbyists.

## Status

Repo: `tbelskie/writing-quest`

Current read: active paid beta candidate with Tauri, React, Rust, SQLite, project creation, project library, Tiptap editor, quest sessions, local persistence, autosave/recovery states, TXT/Markdown export, companion choice, and staged companion evolution. Needs launch-readiness polish before it can be sold confidently.

## Current Bet

Turn Writing Quest into the portfolio's first polished paid launch candidate.

## Launch Lessons

- paid desktop app launch
- seasonal writing challenge timing
- one-time license positioning
- local-first trust
- UX writing for creative flow
- export and persistence reliability

## Known Risks

- final art quality is not yet good enough to carry the emotional promise
- desktop save/export trust still needs beta soak with real writing sessions
- DOCX/EPUB should not be promised until production-valid
- license/trial behavior is undefined and could become a distraction
- Windows installer verification is still outstanding
- launch window requires discipline

## Next Actions

1. Polish the core writing loop UX copy.
2. Make export positioning honest: TXT/Markdown first, DOCX/EPUB later.
3. Create production art direction and asset requirements.
4. Decide paid beta distribution and licensing scope.
5. Draft landing page, privacy/local-first copy, and launch posts.
