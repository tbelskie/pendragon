# Dot Primer Product Brief

## One-Liner

Dot Primer helps people start and protect focused deep work sessions from the browser.

## User

People who work in the browser and need a simple focus ritual without a heavy productivity system.

## Current Evidence

- Passive installs.
- Persistent active usage.
- Low uninstall count.
- Strong Windows skew in observed data.

## Current Bet

The product may solve a real small itch. Audit the repo and store listing before deciding whether to invest.
