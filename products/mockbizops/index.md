# MockBizOps

Realistic fake business APIs for serious prototypes, demos, tutorials, and docs.

## Status

Repo: `tbelskie/mockbizops`

Current read: real FastAPI/Postgres backend with auto-shop data, docs site, deployment files, and an emerging multi-domain architecture. Needs cleanup after drift between docs, routes, seeding, and deployment assumptions.

## Current Bet

MockBizOps can become the portfolio's developer-facing product and docs-as-code testbed.
