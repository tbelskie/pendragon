# MockBizOps Product Brief

## One-Liner

MockBizOps provides realistic fake business APIs for serious prototypes, dashboards, tutorials, demos, and docs.

## User

Frontend developers, indie hackers, tutorial creators, technical writers, design engineers, and AI app builders who need believable business data.

## Problem

Most free fake APIs are toy-grade. They are fine for tutorials, but weak for real SaaS-style dashboards, workflows, and docs.

## Promise

Give builders realistic, relational business data without making them build a backend first.

## Current Evidence

- Working repo existed and was hosted previously.
- FastAPI/Postgres implementation exists.
- Auto-shop domain is substantial.
- Marketing domain shows scenario-pack direction.

## Current Bet

One excellent public demo domain plus strong docs can prove whether developers care.
