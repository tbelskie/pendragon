# Vinegar Product Brief

## One-Liner

Vinegar adds a helpful pause before impulse shopping turns into regret.

## User

People who want to spend more intentionally but do not want a judgmental finance app.

## Problem

Online shopping makes impulse purchases frictionless. Existing tools often focus on budgeting after the fact instead of interrupting the moment of temptation.

## Promise

Create a small, private moment of friction before checkout.

## Current Bet

The product works only if the friction feels supportive.
