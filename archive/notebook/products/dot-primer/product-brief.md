# Dot Primer Product Brief

## One-Liner

Dot Primer helps people start and protect focused deep work sessions from the browser.

## User

People who work in the browser and need a simple focus ritual without a heavy productivity system.

## Current Evidence

- Passive installs.
- Persistent active usage.
- Low uninstall count.
- Strong Windows skew in observed data.
- Public site appears configured for `dotprimer.com`.
- Shipped Chrome extension bundle exists at version 1.0.

## Current Bet

The product may solve a real small itch. Audit the repo and store listing before deciding whether to invest.

## Audit Notes

Dot should probably be treated as a shipped extension first and a future mobile app second. The next cleanup should establish a single source of truth for the extension package, archive old submission folders, and separate the public website from experimental app code.
