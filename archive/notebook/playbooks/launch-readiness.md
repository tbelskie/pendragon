# Launch Readiness

Use this when a product is close enough to launch that polish, positioning, docs, pricing, and feedback loops matter.

## Checks

- Buyer/user is specific.
- Offer is concrete.
- Price or free strategy is explicit.
- First-run experience works.
- Core workflow works without hand-holding.
- Landing page explains the product clearly.
- Docs/help answer predictable questions.
- Support/contact path exists.
- Privacy/trust concerns are addressed.
- Launch channel is chosen.
- Success metric is defined.
- Post-launch review is scheduled.

## Output

- readiness verdict
- top risks
- missing assets
- next five actions
- launch/no-launch recommendation
