## Design Notes

- Keep the surface quieter than the warroom.
- Make the CTA visible, but label it as generated strategy rather than a real active purchase action.
- Treat trust assets as first-class launch material.
- Do not hide blocked docs behind polished copy.

## Why This Matters

Solo founders do not need another place to admire their roadmap.

They need to know:

- what page to publish
- what the page should say
- what the page cannot honestly claim yet
- what must change before strangers can pay
