# Pendragon As Meta-Product

Pendragon belongs in its own portfolio.

It should be tracked as a product because it has its own beta readiness, launch decisions, docs surface, risks, and eventual revenue path.

It should not be treated as just another equal portfolio bet.

## Role

Pendragon is the meta-product.

Its job is to:

- track its own progress toward beta
- prove the launch-room workflow against Writing Quest
- reveal what the platform needs next
- avoid hiding strategic decisions in chat history

## Guardrail

Writing Quest remains the primary proof object.

Pendragon work must make Writing Quest easier to launch. If Pendragon work does not improve the Writing Quest launch path, it waits.

## Meta-Product Risk

The main risk is building meta-infrastructure instead of shipping revenue-generating products.

The product room should keep that risk visible:

> Building meta-infrastructure instead of shipping revenue-generating products.

## Beta Readiness Question

Pendragon is not ready for outside founder beta just because it looks good.

The key decision is:

> When is Pendragon ready for outside founder beta?

Suggested readiness trigger:

> Writing Quest can be planned, documented, and launched from Pendragon without reverting to the notebook.

## Portfolio Rule

Pendragon gets a room.

Writing Quest stays in command.
