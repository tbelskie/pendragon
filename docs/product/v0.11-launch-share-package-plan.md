# v0.11 Launch Share Package Plan

Forge can now generate a launch surface and judge whether that surface is publishable. The next step is to hand the founder the actual copy they can use when they are ready to talk to people.

The principle:

> A founder should leave Forge with something they can send.

## Product Bet

Solo founders lose momentum when a generated page still leaves them staring at blank outreach, launch post, and proof checklist docs.

Launch Share Package turns the product room into a small launch packet:

- primary launch post
- short teaser
- founder DM
- clear-first blockers
- proof links
- next moves
- Markdown export

This makes Forge feel less like a page preview and more like a launch command station.

## v0.11 Scope

Add a Launch Share Package to Forge:

- generate three copy blocks from the launch surface
- allow each block to be copied
- export the package as Markdown
- include QA blockers and proof links in the package
- keep the package grounded in the same surface model as the preview and HTML export

## Non-Goals

Do not add:

- social scheduling
- email sending
- CRM workflows
- analytics
- AI rewrite controls
- channel-specific growth playbooks
- publishing automation

Those are later. v0.11 is a local launch packet.

## Writing Quest Dogfood Standard

Writing Quest needs a packet that says:

- this is not ready to share wide yet
- the CTA URL is missing
- critical docs are still open
- the readiness gate is blocked
- the beta proof link is attached

That is more valuable than polished copy that hides risk.

## Success Criteria

- Forge shows Launch Share Package after Launch Surface QA.
- The package includes launch post, short teaser, and founder DM blocks.
- Copy buttons update status when they succeed or fail.
- Markdown export downloads a complete share package.
- The package includes clear-first blockers, QA checks, proof links, and next moves.
